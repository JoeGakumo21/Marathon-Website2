How to Start Running

You want to know how to start running? We're glad you asked.

Shoes that don't fit well can lead to discomfort, blister or more serious injuries, so it's important to find the right pair.

Your running shows won't fit the same way as your casual shoes, and the size of your feet changes over time, so don't rely on how your shoes fit in the past. So, measure your feet. A local running store will take accurate dynamic measurements to get you into the right shoe.
